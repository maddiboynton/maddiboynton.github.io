About Us

At Mads Pizza, everything begins with family. Our story was born right here on the Central Coast—on beaches like the one in this photo—where gathering together, laughing loudly, and sharing good food have always been the heartbeat of our lives. What started as a family tradition of cooking, experimenting with dough, and perfecting homemade pizzas slowly grew into something bigger: a dream to share that same love of food and togetherness with the San Luis Obispo community.

For us, pizza isn’t just a meal—it’s a reason to gather. It’s late-night conversations around the table, sandy feet after a day at the beach, kids running around the kitchen, and that warm moment when everyone falls quiet because the first slice is just that good. Those moments have shaped our family, and they’re the experiences we hope to bring to every person who visits Mads Pizza.

Mads was created from a simple belief: that great food brings people together. Every recipe on our menu was inspired by family traditions, shared memories, and the joy of creating something delicious with the people you love. We pour that same care into every pizza we make—fresh ingredients, handcrafted dough, and flavors meant to be enjoyed with the ones who matter most.

Today, our mission is to share that love of food and family with our SLOcal community. Whether you're a student grabbing a slice between classes, a family celebrating a special night out, or a visitor discovering the magic of SLO for the first time, we want Mads Pizza to feel like home. From our family to yours—welcome. Pull up a chair, grab a slice, and stay awhile.
